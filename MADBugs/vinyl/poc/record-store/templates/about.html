About the shop

Groove Therapy started in 2019 as a stall at Brighton's Upper Gardner Street market. Seven years later we're still just three people in a small shop on Kerridge Lane — but the catalogue has grown.

We focus on records we'd want to own ourselves: clean first pressings, well-cut reissues, and a shelf of rarities for the people who know what they're after. If you're looking for something specific we don't have, email us — we probably know where to find it.

Every order ships in a custom mailer with a free inner sleeve. We replace any record that arrives damaged, no questions asked.
